<h4
id="1在-cusersadministratorssh-文件夹中创建一个config文件无扩展名填入以下内容">1、在
<code>C:\Users\Administrator\.ssh</code>
文件夹中创建一个config文件（无扩展名），填入以下内容：</h4>
<pre><code>Host github.com
    HostName github.com
    PreferredAuthentications publickey
    IdentityFile ~/.ssh/id_rsa

# lingxiu58(自定义)
Host lingxiu58.github.com
    HostName github.com
    PreferredAuthentications publickey
    IdentityFile ~/.ssh/id_rsa_lingxiu58

# lingxiu5858(自定义，我习惯和账户名保持一致)
Host lingxiu5858.gitee.com
    HostName gitee.com
    PreferredAuthentications publickey
    IdentityFile ~/.ssh/id_rsa_lingxiu5858</code></pre>
